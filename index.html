At Royal Rasoi, we serve the rich culinary heritage of Kerala — from the iconic Sadhya on fresh banana leaves every afternoon, to crispy Karimeen Pollichathu and silky Appam with stew. Every dish is crafted with authentic recipes and traditional spices.
